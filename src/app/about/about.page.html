About KalaSanman

Founded on an auspicious Maharashtra Day on May 1st 2019, KalaSanman is a platform for Performing Arts. KalaSanman....To Respect and Showcase the Performing Arts of the Greater Cincinnati Area Performers!!! KalaSanman provides platform for performance of a play, dance, recitals, music, or any other performing art by local artists!!!
